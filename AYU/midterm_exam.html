Problem 1

You are given the following summary statistics:

\[\begin{align*} n &= 5 \\ \sum{x} &= 26 \\ \sum{y} &= 68 \\ \sum (x_i-\bar{x})^2 &= 64.8 \\ \sum (x_i - \bar{x})(y_i-\bar{y}) &= 156.4 \\ \sum (y_i-\bar{y})^2 &= 401.2 \end{align*}\]

Determine the equation of the regression line, using the least squares method.

  1. \(y = 1.049 + 2.414x\)

  2. \(y = 1.049 - 2.414x\)

  3. \(y = -2.414 - 1.049x\)

  4. \(y = 2.414x - 1.049x\)

  5. The correct answer is not given by (A), (B), (C), or (D).
